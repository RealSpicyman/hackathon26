About Us

What is AddressMe? AddressMe is a tool that makes property research and evaluation in Philadelphia easier. Enter an address and we search 900+ leasable properties in our dataset to provide a letter grade and key property metrics.

Engineered by intelligence: AddressMe also uses an AI model to assess properties that are not listed in our database. The model uses geolocation and relative crime-rate context to generate a reliable letter grade.

    How to read these metrics

    • Energy Star Score: Federal 1-100 efficiency score for many building types. Higher is better.
    • Composite Score: Weighted environmental performance score on a 0-100 scale. Higher is better.
    • EUI Percentile: Relative energy use intensity compared to similar properties (0-100).
    • GHG Percentile: Relative greenhouse gas performance compared to peers (0-100).
    • Water Percentile: Relative water efficiency compared to similar properties (0-100).
